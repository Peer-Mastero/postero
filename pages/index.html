What you stand to gain

Get the most out of your university experience with our platform.

Peer-to-peer learning

The platform enables peer-to-peer learning, which can be a more effective way of learning for some students. Learning from peers who have recently acquired the same skill can provide more relatable and digestible learning experiences than learning from a traditional teacher.

Access to quality learning resources

The platform provides access to quality learning resources for university students who may not have access to expensive external skill development programs or private tutors.
